Javascript/Front-end Mentoring Program

Training Program

This tutorial project was developed as part of the free online course "Javascript/Front-end Mentoring Program (in English)" from The Rolling Scopes community.

The training consists of 3 stages. There are strict requirements for the transition to each higher stage. This particular course of study takes place in the form of self-study. This means that students have access to pre-assessment of webinars and two-week live Q&A sessions with course trainers. Organizers provide students with a list of topics that must be covered for each stage with recommended deadlines.

After accomplishing all three stages of education, students will receive an electronic certificate of completion.
